Sustainabilty

At SGI, sustainability is at the
heart of our business
underpinning every decision
we make.

Automotive Aggregation

Our vehicles range from electric vehicles that produce zero emissions to hybrid models that combine fuel efficiency with reduced environmental impact, thereby having something for every eco-conscious driver. We equally understand the importance of reducing carbon footprint in Africa, and are committed to paving the way forward, towards a greener future. In adopting a sustainable approach to our Autotech business for the greater good of Africa and her environment, we stand out in the value that is a differential needed in today's market. Our objective for this line of business is optimal performance and an eco-conscious environment. We proactively embrace a clean environment as we evolve and transition towards other sustainable energy alternatives.

Media Production

Advox helps its clients to create compelling and captivating media content that showcases their brands, products or services in a professional and appealing way. Advox offers media production services such as TV and radio commercial production, video editing, product photography, animation, voice over and more. Advox also has a state-of-the-art studio that is equipped with the latest technology and equipment to ensure the best quality output for the clients' media projects.

Agricultural Export

SGI is dedicated to driving eco-friendly innovations in farming. Our core focus is delivering top-notch eco-friendly Agro export services tailored to diverse client needs. Whether expanding market reach or streamlining supply chains, our green solutions are designed to help you achieve goals responsibly. Our unwavering commitment to excellence ensures we lead the competition, maximizing success in this fast-paced, sustainable industry.
