Steveraton About us Leadership

The Group Managing Director

Dear visionaries of tomorrow, as the Group Managing Director of Steveraton Group Incorporated (SGI), I am thrilled to share our journey with you. Nestled in the heart of Nigeria, SGI is a beacon of technological innovation and progress, with influential ties spanning across Europe, America, and Asia. Ours is a journey that is fueled by the integrity, knowledge, creativity, skill, diversity, and teamwork of our dedicated employees - qualities we hold in the highest regard. For me, delivering excellent service is not just a catchphrase; it's a deeply ingrained belief. It's like chasing the horizon - once you reach one milestone, another emerges on the horizon. It is with this relentless pursuit of excellence that I founded Steveraton Group Incorporated (SGI), a conglomerate with seven active subsidiaries and more on the way championing technology and innovations. These subsidiaries span across various sectors including: Autobuy, our trailblazing subsidiary, is steering the future of transportation with its autonomous self-driving technologies. We're not just changing how we move -we're making every journey safer and more efficient. Advox is more than an advertising agency - it's a platform for connection. We're not just promoting products; we're sparking meaningful conversations and building bridges between brands and people. And with future innovations under research, we're set to redefine the advertising landscape. In the financial sphere, Silvine is breaking barriers by leveraging alternative financial means and capital markets through bank assets and securitization. Our mission? To democratise finance and make it accessible to all. With Trance-Agro, we're turning the tide on global food production and export challenges. By harnessing futuristic agricultural innovations, we aim to empower the world in food production and eradicate hunger in Nigeria. Brainiac, the technological prodigy of SGI, is constantly pushing boundaries. Our think-tank of maverick thinkers are shattering the status quo with ground-breaking innovations like holographic technology, nanotechnology and artificial intelligence in healthcare, military, aviation, energy, and 3D printing. With each challenge we have faced, my brilliant team and I have emerged stronger and more determined. This resilience has been instrumental in propelling Steveraton Group Incorporated to new heights of global recognition. The journey of Steveraton Group Incorporated and its subsidiaries is an ongoing one. But with the unwavering support of my team and clients, I am confident that we will continue to scale new peaks of excellence for decades to come.

Our Purpose

Our purpose extends beyond our own growth; our relentless pursuit to challenge and overcome technological barriers in Africa and globally led to the birth of SGI. It is an envisioned platform to cultivate innovative ideas and imaginations, reaching beyond industry boundaries and geographical limits

TECHNOLOGY

Technology is our core essence as a company and a key driver of growth across various industries.

We are here to revolutionise the way businesses operate, leading to increased efficiency, productivity, and access.

INNOVATION

Innovation is a fundamental cornerstone that SGI relies on for its continual progress, whether today or in decades to come.

In embracing complexities, we encourage businesses to uncover unique opportunities. We are committed to understanding the intricate dynamics of all industries in our focus.

GROWTH

Our growth strategy is underpinned by our commitment to innovation, operational excellence and customer satisfaction.

We believe that by focusing on these areas, we can expand our market share, diversify our revenue streams, and enhance our profitability. Our growth projection is not just about increasing our bottom line but also about creating a positive impact on the communities we serve.

TRADE

We are committed to bringing inclusivity to commerce. We believe in the power of trade to drive economic growth and cross-class alliances, create jobs and build stronger communities.

Our goal is to enable businesses of all sizes to connect, compete, and thrive in the global marketplace. We are committed to bringing an inclusive culture to commerce

SUSTAINABILITY

The future matters to us at SGI. Sustainability is a key factor in securing the future of our environment and the company. It involves implementing strategies that

fulfil the company’s current needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their full potential.

NAVIGATE COMPLEXITIES

We are aware of the instability in many business environments, thanks to our well-honed vision. We can, however, navigate these complexities with resilience, agility, data and foresight.

We are committed to understanding the intricate dynamics of any industry we focus our attention on, anticipating the market trends there, and making informed decisions that drive growth. By embracing complexity, we are encouraged to uncover unique opportunities, foster innovation and create value for our stakeholders. 'Navigating Complexities' is not just our purpose; it's our strategic advantage in the face of uncertainty. This is how we chart a course towards a prosperous future for our stakeholders, partners and subsidiaries.
